About System

It might have happened so many times that you or someone yours need doctor’s help immediately, but they are not available due to some reasons.The Disease Prediction system is an end user support and online consultation project. Here, we propose a web application that allow users to get instant guidance on their disease through an intelligent system. The application is fed with various details and the disease associated with those details.The application allows user to share their disease related issues.It then processes user specific details to check for various illness that could be associated with it. Based on result, patient can contact doctor accordingly for further treatment.
